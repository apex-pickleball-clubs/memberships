Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in an Apex Pickleball membership?

Apex memberships include unlimited court access to all 16 indoor courts, priority booking privileges (14 days advance), guest passes for friends and family, pro shop discounts (15-20% off), one free intro class for guests, PickleDilly's restaurant benefits, and invitations to members-only events and social mixers.

Is a membership worth it compared to drop-in play?

If you play 2-3 times per week, a membership typically pays for itself within the first month when compared to hourly court rental rates ($15-25/hour). Members also enjoy priority booking during peak hours, which is valuable for Cedar Park's busy pickleball community. Additionally, the pro shop discounts and PickleDilly's benefits provide extra value beyond court access alone.

Can I bring guests with my membership?

Yes! All Apex memberships include guest passes each month. Individual memberships include 2 monthly passes, Couples get 4, and Family memberships receive 8. Unused passes do not roll over month-to-month, so we encourage members to share pickleball with friends and family regularly.

What's the cancellation policy?

Apex offers month-to-month memberships with no long-term contracts. You can cancel anytime with 30 days' notice. Annual memberships (which offer discounted rates) require a one-year commitment but can be frozen for up to 60 days per year for medical reasons or extended travel.

Do you offer corporate or group memberships?

Yes! We offer customized corporate membership packages for Austin-area businesses looking to provide employee wellness benefits. Group rates are available for organizations with 10+ members. Contact our membership team for a custom quote and benefits package.
